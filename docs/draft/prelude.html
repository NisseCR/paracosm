Prologue · Session I

The Ashes of Valdrun

A tale of ruin, vengeance, and what endures the flame

Scroll

Once, they said Valdrun would never fall. Its towers were carved from the bones of the earth, its gates sealed by oaths older than the kingdom itself. The people slept easy beneath its shadow, and the shadow was kind.

That was before the fire. Before the silence that followed the fire.

You were not there when it burned. Perhaps that is the only reason you still breathe. But now the road behind you is closed, your debts are many, and the last courier you spoke to — three days ago, one arrow in his shoulder and two in his horse — pressed a crumpled letter into your hands and said only: "Find what they were looking for. Before they come back."

The town of Embers Cross lies half a day's ride ahead. What remains of Valdrun's people have gathered there — or so the rumours claim. The truth, as always, will cost you something.

Your story begins now.
